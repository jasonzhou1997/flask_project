About the Site

Hi, I am a recent McGill University graduate and am currently doing my Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering at University of Michigan.

This site has two simple engineering apps I made using python flask:

1. The Composite Mat Calculator is an input & output program to enable design based on computing laminate stiffness and compliance matrix, and perform failure analysis for Composite Laminates and honeycomb structures. The work is based on the course project I completed in MECH 530 Mechanics of Composite Materials in Fall 2019.

2. The ADI heat calculator solves the 2D diffusion of Heat equation over a squared plate with Dirichlet Boundary Conditions using the Alternating-Direction Implicit (ADI) Scheme.
